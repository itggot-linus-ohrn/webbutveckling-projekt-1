History

John Jurasek was born June 21, 1997 in Southern New York State. Not a lot is known about his past but his youtube career started in 2011 where he would record himself reviewing different energy drinks. He called this review series the Energy Crisis and he has since then started reviewing other stuff like fast or premade food. That video series is called Running On Empty and this is his most famous serie up to date. He has also reviewed some special drinks like the pomegranate-flavored 5h Energy drink but this series does not have an official name.

In his past John Jurasek has taken part in the Needle Drop podcast and even had a small cameo in tosh-O. He has also hosted his own shortwave radio show called VORW Radio International where he would talk and play viewer requested music.
