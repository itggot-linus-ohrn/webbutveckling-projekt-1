Who is John Jurasek?

John Jurasek more commonly known as TheReportOfTheWeek or just Reviewbrah. Born on June 21 1997 in Southern New York State. He is well known for reviewing food from various large fast food chains such as McDonalds, Burger king, Taco bell. Starting food reviews at the young age of 13 John has become one of the most beloved food reviewers on the entire internet.
